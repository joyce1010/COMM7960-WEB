Iceland Landmark

Reykjavik （DT）





  • Reykjavik, the capital and largest city of Iceland, located in the southwestern part of the country, facing the Atlantic Ocean.
  • Famous for its clean energy usage, with nearly all buildings heated by geothermal energy, making it one of the cleanest and most eco-friendly cities in the world.
  • An ideal starting point for exploring other parts of Iceland, including popular tourist destinations like the Golden Circle, with its geysers, waterfalls, and national parks.
  • A prime location for viewing the “Northern Lights”, especially during the winter months.
